Festive Distribution

The most beautiful part of any festival is the joy of sharing that is associated with it. With Durga Puja lighting everyone up this Durga Puja season, there are many who are still deprived of the joy. This NGO based in Kolkata city is making sure that the colours of the festival reach the deprived ones as well. They are distributing food to the street dwellers, the homeless and the needy and spreading the pomp of the festivities.

Mid day meal

Under the mid-day meal scheme, a wholesome meal is the right of all the children in the 6 to 14 age group. Donate to make the nutritious meal a reality for million of poor children in India. It not only provides at least one healthy meal to the children who otherwise have no other source of a proper meal but also encourages parents to send their kids to school more regularly. Help improve the nutritional level and attendance of kids from the disadvantageous society and help achieve two Sustainable Development Goals: Zero Hunger and Quality
